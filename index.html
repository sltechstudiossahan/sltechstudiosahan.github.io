R. M. Sahan Lakmina is a Sri Lankan web designer, web developer, and keyboardist. He is the founder and owner of SL Tech Studios. He is known as a real legend who does not try to be famous, focusing instead on skills, creativity, and continuous learning.

Early Life and Education

Sahan Lakmina was born on 25 April 2011 in Sri Lanka. He studied at Sri Rahula College, Anuradhapura. From an early age, he showed exceptional interest in computers, technology, and digital creation.

Career

Sahan started his journey in the IT field as a self-taught developer. Over the years, he gained experience in:

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Web UI/UX Design
  • Thumbnail Design
  • Photo Editing
  • Video Editing

He later founded SL Tech Studios, a digital brand offering services in web development, graphic design, and multimedia.

Music Career

Along with technology, Sahan is also a professional-level keyboardist. He uses digital tools to combine music and technology for creative production.
